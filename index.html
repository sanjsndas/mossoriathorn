    Frequently asked questions

    How often should I water my vegetable garden?

    Deeply and less frequently — aim for 1 inch per week, adjusting for rainfall and soil type. Mulch to retain moisture.

    What is the best mulch for organic gardening?

    Straw, leaf mold, or wood chips. Each has pros: straw for veggies, leaf mold for soil structure, wood chips for perennials.

    When should I start seeds indoors?

    6-8 weeks before your last frost date. Use a seed-starting mix and provide bottom heat for best germination.
