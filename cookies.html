Your data, our promise

We believe in transparency. This policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information when you visit our garden — digital or physical.

Our commitment

At Mossoria Thorn Whisper, we respect your privacy and are committed to protecting your personal data. This privacy policy will inform you about how we look after your personal data when you visit our website (regardless of where you visit it from) and tell you about your privacy rights and how the law protects you.

🌿 Summary: We collect only what we need, we never sell your data, and you can opt out at any time. If you have questions, our contact details are at the end of this page.

Information we collect

▸ personal data

Identity & contact

When you subscribe, fill a form, or email us, we may collect your name, email address, and any details you share in your message.

▸ technical data

Usage & analytics

We gather anonymous data about your visit — pages viewed, time spent, and browser type — to improve our content and experience.

▸ marketing

Newsletter preferences

If you opt in, we store your email to send you seasonal almanacs and garden stories. You can unsubscribe with one click.

How we use your data

🌱 To provide our service

We use your contact details to respond to inquiries, send you requested information, and manage your newsletter subscription. We never share your data with third parties for marketing.

📈 To improve our garden

Analytics help us understand which articles resonate most, so we can grow better content. All data is aggregated and anonymised.

We keep your data only as long as necessary and protect it with industry-standard measures.

Your rights

Right to access & rectification

You can request a copy of the data we hold about you, and ask us to correct any inaccuracies.

Right to erasure

You can ask us to delete your personal data at any time. We will do so promptly, unless we are required to keep it for legal reasons.

Right to withdraw consent

If you’ve subscribed to our newsletter, you can unsubscribe using the link in every email, or by contacting us directly.

Cookies & tracking

We use only essential cookies to keep the site running smoothly. No third-party tracking cookies are used without your explicit consent. You can control cookie settings in your browser.

🍪 Our cookie policy: We use a minimal set of cookies — just enough to remember your preferences and gather anonymous analytics. We do not use tracking for advertising.
